# ContinuMail

**Practical mail tooling for the people who actually run the mail.**

*Conversion · archival · automation — built by a technician, for technicians.*

</div>

---

## What this is

**ContinuMail** is a growing catalogue of focused tools for mail migration,
archival, and the unglamorous plumbing that technicians deal with every day —
the kind of jobs where the "official" answer is *"just install Outlook and
click through this 14-step wizard."*

Each tool does one job, does it without forcing you into a vendor's ecosystem,
and ships only once it's actually been polished. No bloat, no forced cloud,
no half-finished betas dressed up as products.

## Who's behind it

I'm **Aksel Visby** — an MSP technician and hobby developer, building this with
my ever-patient AI sidekick, **Claude**. I bring the real-world MSP scars (and
the questionable ideas); Claude brings the typing speed and the patience to
refactor them into something that actually works.

ContinuMail is the home for the utilities I kept wishing existed in the middle
of real jobs: mailbox migrations, archive conversions, one-off scripts that
became something worth keeping. Rather than letting them rot in a scratch
folder, I'm cleaning them up and releasing them here, one at a time.

## The vision

A dependable, slowly-growing toolbox of **technician-grade mail and archival
utilities** — scripts and applications that respect your time, your data, and
your existing setup. Built from the trenches of MSP work, not a product roadmap
in a boardroom.

ContinuMail follows an **open-core** model: the core, "wedge" tools stay free
and open source — forever. They're genuinely useful on their own. More
polished, paid products may grow alongside them, but the free tools aren't
bait-and-switch crippleware.

## In the catalogue

- 🗂️ **ContinuMail Converter** — convert `mbox` mail archives to Outlook `PST`,
  **without Outlook or any Microsoft library installed.** Free & open source
  (GPL-3.0-or-later). → [`continumail-converter`](https://github.com/ContinuMail/continumail-converter)

*More mail, archival, and automation tools are in the workshop — released
gradually as each one earns its release.*

## How it's built

- **By a tech, for techs** — opinionated toward the realities of MSP work.
- **Honest about scope** — tools say what they do and what they don't.
- **Released when ready** — gradual and polished, not rushed.
- **Open where it counts** — free core tooling stays free.
